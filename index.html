welcome to quy nhon

When we at Maia think of “getting away from it all”, Maia Resort Quy Nhon is the kind of place we imagine. With private pools and secluded gardens outside each and every one of our spacious thatched villas, plus stunning beach and riverfront views, mouth-watering dining options, a “breakfast anywhere, anytime” concept, and Maia’s unique “all-spa inclusive” service, it’s the ideal place to disconnect, switch off, and put the rest of the world on hold for a few days or weeks at a time.
