where dreams & emotions live

VỊ Restaurant

Vị is the resort’s culinary centrepiece, and with views of Quy Nhon Bay’s shimmering waters and a refreshing sea breeze it’s the perfect place to grab a bite, or just sit back, relax and enjoy the view. The restaurants live cooking stations showcase the best of the region’s local produce and bountiful seafood, and with a warm and welcoming atmosphere, this is the ideal setting for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Ad Hoc Restaurant

Ad Hoc Restaurant

Capturing the resort’s culinary identity with dishes of pared-back simplicity, Ad Hoc’s chefs visit the local markets daily, sourcing the freshest seasonal ingredients before returning to the kitchen and crafting them into a unique, daily changing menu designed for a communal dining experience. The restaurant’s offers an ideal setting for dishes prepared for sharing with friends and family.

Pool Bar

Pool Bar

As another day of blissful relaxation or exploring of the surrounding region comes to an end, what better place to relax and watch the sun sink slowly behind the horizon, and enjoy an ice cold beer or a signature handcrafted cocktails. With comfortable seating and a creatively enticing décor, Beach Bar provides a place to reflect on the day’s events and plan for tomorrow’s.

Culinary journey

Culinary journey

Maia Resorts introduce Vietnamese culture through amazing culinary journeys, creating mouth‑watering “wow” moments throughout the stay. From cooking classes with hand‑picked ingredients from the resort’s organic greenhouses, to visiting local farms and dining with farming families, catching seafood with local fishermen, or discovering hidden foodie pop-ups around the resort, each experience is designed to blend an ethos of wellbeing with an incomparable culinary experience.
